Get Started

Welcome to the Catalouge of Federal Domestic Assistance website, your gateway to financial assistance and support specifically designed for retirees. The Catalouge of Federal Domestic Assistance is dedicated to providing grants to retirees, empowering them to navigate financial hardships, pursue personal endeavors, and maintain a high quality of life during their retirement years.

Financial Assistance for Retirees

The CFDA offers a range of grant programs tailored to meet the diverse needs of retirees.

How Does It Work?
Click "Get Started Now," answer a few questions relating to your financial situation, the purpose for which you seek assistance and some necessary information about yourself.

Once your application is complete, our dedicated team is available to answer any questions you may have during the application process, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience.

We review and score every application and give recommendations even if you are not selected!
